Why Choose Cuteland?

Healthy Meals Included

Homemade meals 3-4 times a day. We cook with fresh ingredients and follow state nutrition guidelines. Your kid will actually eat vegetables here.

Small Group Sizes

Maximum 9 children. That's it. Your child won't get lost in a crowd. We know every kid's favorite snack, their nap schedule, and when they need a hug.

Family Atmosphere

We're a home daycare, not a facility. Kids call us by first name, help set the table, and actually want to come back on Monday mornings.

Our Mission

When we started Cuteland Daycare, we had one goal: make it the kind of place WE would want for our own kids. A "home away from home" that feels warm, safe, and fun. Your child won't just be watched here - they'll be cared for, played with, and celebrated every single day.
